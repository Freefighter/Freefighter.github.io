Hey, I am Yifan Chen, a Stats PhD student at UIUC, advised by Prof. Yun Yang.

My research interest lies in the general area of computation in machine learning, currently in neural network acceleration. I focus on the models with heavy matrix operation, including Transformers and graph convolutional networks (GCNs).

I obtained my B.S. degree from Department of Statistics, Fudan University in 2018, where I received some preparatory research training advised by Prof. Juan Shen and Prof. Chenghong Zhang.
